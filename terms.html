The gist:

We run a few services collectively known as ValidFIX.com and would love for you to use them. They are designed to give you the ability to analyze, validate, understand, control, what goes on in your FIX messages.

We understand that FIX messages might contain sensitive data and therefore we do not send them across the network. The processing of FIX messages, by any of our services, happens only on your browser on your machine.
