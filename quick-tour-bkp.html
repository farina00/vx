  • What is www.ValidFIX.com ?

    ValidFIX is a website for anybody who works with the FIX Protocol.

    For the business, we have VX: an enterprise-class web solution that gives you rich insights into your financial data.

    For the developers, we have two simple cloud-based services:

    1. FIX Analyzer to inspect one single fix message
    2. FIX Log Analyzer to inspect sections of logs from any FIX Application

  • What about sensitive data ?

    We don't encrypt your data to protect it: we have figured the safest way to protect is NOT to send it at all.

    All our services are running on your machine. Your FIX messages are not sent over the network.

    In other words: You And Your firm have really nothing to worry about.
